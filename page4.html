School-Aged Programs

Crafting

There's so many craft programming that can occur at the library. It provides children an oppotunity to be artisitc and allows for free expression.
Craft programs can also be learning moments. For example, a craft program may consist of teaching how to crochet or knit, or how to make a candle. The possibilities are endless!

Book Clubs

Book clubs are very popular amongst school-aged children. Reading for fun helps develop literacy skills. Addiotnally, book clubs offer an oppotunity for children to interact with one another and share ideas in a no judgement zone. Graphic novel book clubs are a new type of book club, that is increasing in popularity.
Here are resources for three different types of book clubs:

  • Little Reader's Book Club
  • Graphic Novel Book Club
  • Elementary Novel Book Club

STEM Programs

STEM stands for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. STEM library programs helps enhance critical thinking skills, and allows chlidren to use their imagination in a place outside of school.
Here are some resources pertaining to STEM programming:
