Programming for Toddlers

Choosing Books for Toddlers and Preschoolers

  • Books that introduce or reinforce concepts (colors, shapes etc)
  • Books that show everyday activities
  • Books with audience participation
  • Books with a clear narrative
  • Books that are funny
  • Books that are complex
  • Books that reinforce a concept (colors, shapes, etc)
  • Books with a surprise element
